Travel Plan

Simple Travel Plan to North East

  • Day 1: Arrive in Guwahati, Assam
  • Day 2: Explore Kamakhya Temple and Umananda Island
  • Day 3: Travel to Shillong, Meghalaya
  • Day 4: Visit Elephant Falls and Shillong Peak
  • Day 5: Drive to Cherrapunji, explore Nohkalikai Falls and Mawsmai Caves
  • Day 6: Head to Kaziranga National Park, Assam for wildlife safari
  • Day 7: Explore Majuli Island, the world's largest river island
  • Day 8: Depart from Dibrugarh Airport
